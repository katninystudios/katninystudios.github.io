The Future of Meowser

By @katniny

Published: 2/28/25

Updated: Never



Once upon a time, there was a browser named Meowser. It was cute, it was fun, and well... it also sounded like a certain Nintendo character with a catty appearance.

While I love a good cat pun, I figured it was tie for something more distinct, recognizaable, and let's be honest - less likely to result in a cease-and-desist letter from Nintendo.

So, say hello to Kairo!


Why the Change?

Meowser was always meant to be a browser that put users first, with privacy, customization, and control at its core. But as I kept working on it, I realized that the current codebase wasn't just messy, it was holding the project back. Debugging was difficult, adding new features felt like duct-taping a spaceship together, and at some point, I had to admit... it was time to start fresh.
