About Katniny Studios

Building a better internet, one that puts users first.


Who We Are

Katniny Studios is an independent software development suite focused on privacy, customization, and user freedom. We're tired of corporations controlling the web, tracking users, and prioritizing profit over people, so we're building alternatives.


Our Mission

The modern internet is bloated, invasive, and restrictive. We believe in an internet where:

  • ✅ Users have control over their experience
  • ✅ Software is built with transparency and user choice in mind
  • ✅ Privacy isn't an afterthought, it's the default

What We Create

We develop software that respects user freedom, from web standards to alternative online platforms. Some of our projects include:

  • TransSocial - a privacy-respecting social media platform
  • Kairo - a privacy-respecting, fast, no harmful features web browser

Everything we build follows the same core principles: no unnecessary tracking, no data harvesting, and no compromises.


Our Philosophy

We don't believe privacy should be a premium feature. We don't believe users should have to dig through settings just to disable tracking. We don't believe corporations should decide how people experience the web.
