Taking Back Your Internet Privacy

Because privacy is a right, not a privilege.


The internet was meant to be open, free, and user-controlled—but corporations have turned it into a surveillance machine. Katniny Studios is working to change that by developing software that respects users, prioritizes privacy, and gives you back control of your online experience.


Why choose privacy?

Big Tech thrives on collecting your data, tracking your habits, and selling your information. We believe in an internet where:

  • ❌ Your personal data isn't a product
  • ❌ Invasive ads and trackers don't follow you
  • ❌ Companies don't dictate how you browse

Instead, we focus on creating tools that:

  • ✅ Minimize data collection
  • ✅ Put users first, not advertisers
  • ✅ Are transparent about how they work


Take Control of your Internet

Whether it's alternative web standards, privacy-respecting software, or giving users more customization, Katniny Studios is building an internet that works for you, not against you.
